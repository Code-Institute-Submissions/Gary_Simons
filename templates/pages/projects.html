wildbunch florist

Wild Bunch is a (fictious) high-end florist based in Soho, central London. I wanted to create a strong visual brand for them using beautiful imagery and an upmarket feel. I website uses beautiful images of flowers and clean design to reflect the brand's values. I used a hand written logo font to show the brand's wild and care-free attitude, which I feel would resonate with it's customers who want that little bit of wildness in the middle of the city.

hummingbird family services

This is a website for a fictional family services company. I wanted the user experience to be positive and welcoming, as these services are mostly contacted in times of struggle and unhappiness. The bright hummingbird illustration feels hopeful and joyous with uplifting colours against a white clean calming background. The clean typography adds to the feeling of calm.
